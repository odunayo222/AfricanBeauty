Home Remedies For Your Skincare

Cucumber:

Cucumber is cooling for the skin. It replenishes and rejuvenates dull skin with its antioxidants. It also improves complexion and reduces swelling. The greatest and most important skin benefit of cucumber is that it helps in revitalizing the skin and also heals sunburnt skin and brings instant relief. You Will Need: 1 small cucumber, 2-3 tablespoons yogurt. All you need to do is to grate the cucumber and add the yogurt to it. Beat it properly. Apply it on your face and neck. Leave it on for five minutes until it dries and washes it off with cold water.

Orange Juice:

Orange juice works effectively for shrinking the open pores. Just apply the juice of an orange on your face; let it stay for 2-3 minutes and wash off immediately. This helps in closing the enlarged pores and giving a shining glow to your face. ... Orange is rich in citric acid, which is very helpful in drying away acne. Orange juice detoxifies the body. Its vitamin C content clears your complexion, giving you glowing skin in no time

Banana:

Banana is rich in potassium and moisture, it will hydrate and moisturizes dry skin, making it soft and supple. it also makes the skin soft and glowing and lighten blemishes. If your skin is peeling and dry, simply apply a banana face mask on your face. Get a small bowl, mash up a ripe banana with the back of your fork, and apply to your face, avoiding the area around your eye and mouth.

Yogurt

Yogurt contains lactic acid, an alpha hydroxy acid, that dissolves dead skin cells. This gentle exfoliation not only helps to create a natural glow and prevent breakouts, but works to diminish the appearance of lines and wrinkles When applied on the skin, yogurt helps to remove dark and dead skin cells and also expose a layer of fresh, new cells. Applying plain yogurt on the face and other parts of the skin which has been darkened is very helpful in skin whitening.

Papaya

Papaya contains Vitamin A and Papain enzyme, papaya helps remove dead skin cells and inactive proteins, thus rejuvenating your skin. It also helps keep your skin hydrated. It removes the top layer of dead cells on the face and makes your skin radiant and younger looking. It also clears the dirt and oil that can lead to acne and breakouts on the face. If you want glowing skin, use papaya-honey mask. Mash half a papaya and mix it with three spoons of honey to make a smooth paste.

Coconut Oil

It is one of the best natural home remedies for glowing skin. This oil can also be used as a natural face moisturiser. It helps in nourishing and hydrating the skin as well as the body. It also has soothing properties which help in treating other skin problems such as dry skin, eczema, etc. To get glowing and radiant skin, you need 1 teaspoon of coconut oil, 2 tablespoons of honey, 1 tablespoon of green tea leaves and 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice and mix them all together. Then soak some cotton into it and apply it on your face. Wash your face thoroughly with warm water after leaving it for around 5 to 10 minutes. This would help in giving you a radiant as well as glowing skin. You can apply this face mask 3 or 4 times in a week for getting radiant skin.
