Black is Beautiful

No matter how cliche it may be, black is indeed beautiful. We are Africans, we are strong, powerful,and unique in our ways and nobody can take that away from us. Our skin color is our African pride which is why we are unique. The fact that "you are black does not mean your brain is blank".

Our Objectives

We are passionate about black Africans who have bleached their skin and those who are yet to, but think its the best option. And to create awareness about the side effects of skin bleaching. To also make them realise that bleaching is not fashion but destruction to their skin.
